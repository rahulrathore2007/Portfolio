About Me

Rahul Rathore

I am Rahul Rathore, a dedicated first-year BSc student at Rakesh College, currently immersed in the rigorous preparation for the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE). Having successfully completed my 12th standard in 2023, I decided to pursue my passion for science and mathematics through higher education. Rakesh College, with its reputation for academic excellence and a vibrant learning environment, was the natural choice for me. As I delve into the diverse subjects offered in my BSc curriculum, I find myself captivated by the intricacies of the scientific world. The pursuit of knowledge is not only confined to textbooks; I actively participate in various clubs and events, honing my leadership skills and fostering a sense of camaraderie within the college community.

The JEE preparation journey is an intense and challenging experience that I wholeheartedly embrace. It serves as a crucial stepping stone towards my goal of entering a prestigious institution for further studies in my chosen field. The guidance and support of my professors and peers at Rakesh College have been instrumental in shaping my academic path. As I navigate the complex yet fascinating world of science, I am confident that the comprehensive education and holistic development I receive here will lay a strong foundation for my future endeavors.
